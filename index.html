👁️ LIGHT & VISION

  • Light makes us see — it reveals the world around us
  • The human eye can detect over 10 million colors
  • Light travels from the sun to Earth in 8 minutes and 20 seconds
  • Your eyes can detect a single photon of light!

😊 LIGHT & MOOD

  • Light exposure affects serotonin — the happiness chemical
  • Morning sunlight helps regulate your circadian rhythm
  • Bright light makes us more alert and focused
💛 “Let there be light” — and suddenly, everything feels brighter.

🌱 PHOTOSYNTHESIS

  • Plants convert light energy into chemical energy
  • 6CO₂ + 6H₂O + Light → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂
  • Without light, there is no oxygen — no us

🌍 LIGHT & CLIMATE

  • The sun provides Earth with 174 petawatts of energy
  • Light maintains Earth's habitable temperature
  • Solar energy drives wind and ocean currents
⚡ THE PHYSICS OF LIGHT

📏 WAVE OR PARTICLE?

  • Light behaves as BOTH a wave AND a particle
  • Particles are called "photons"
  • Einstein won Nobel Prize for this discovery

📐 SPEED OF LIGHT

  • 299,792,458 meters per second
  • About 670 million miles per hour
  • Nothing in the universe is faster

🌈 LIGHT SPECTRUM

  • Visible light is a tiny part of EM spectrum
  • ROYGBIV — Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, Violet
  • Different animals see different spectrums

✨ FASCINATING LIGHT FACTS
🐙

Bioluminescence

Over 80% of deep-sea creatures produce their own light through chemical reactions.

🎨

Color is Light

Objects don't have color — they reflect certain wavelengths of light.

⚡

Lightning Speed

A lightning bolt heats the air to 50,000°F — 5x hotter than the sun!

🌙

Moonlight

Moonlight is reflected sunlight — it takes 1.3 seconds to reach Earth.

💎

Diamond Sparkle

Diamonds sparkle because they slow light down more than air.

🦋

Butterfly Wings

Butterfly wings create color through light interference, not pigments!
